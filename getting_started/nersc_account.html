Getting a NERSC Computing Account

On this page

  • I Already Have a NERSC Account

  • I Need a New NERSC Account

I Already Have a NERSC Account

Follow these steps if you already have a NERSC account and need access to the DESC repository.

  1. If you haven’t already, apply to become a DESC Member.

  2. Visit the NERSC website: https://www.nersc.gov/users/accounts/user-accounts/get-a-nersc-account/

  3. Click Create a NERSC Account form.

  4. Click I have a current NERSC account.

  5. Enter your NERSC username.

  6. Search for the project using PI Name by entering Digel and choose the m1727 project, which is LSST_DESC.

  7. Complete the rest of the form and click Ok at the top right of the page.

You will receive email confirmation about your updated status within a few days, possibly sooner.

Note

If you need assistance, contact lsst-desc-opsi-mgmt at slac dot stanford dot edu

Once your account access is confirmed, proceed to Managing a NERSC Account for useful details about managing and setting up your NERSC account.

I Need a New NERSC Account

Step 1 — Apply for an account at NERSC

  1. If you haven’t already, apply to become a DESC Member.

  2. Visit the NERSC website: https://www.nersc.gov/users/accounts/user-accounts/get-a-nersc-account/

  3. Click Create a NERSC Account form.

  4. Click I need a new NERSC account.

  5. NERSC will request your ORCiD. If you do not already have one, you will be asked to create one.

  6. Enter your preferred username.

  7. Search for the project using PI Name by entering Digel and choose the m1727 project, which is LSST_DESC.

  8. Fill in your contact information.

  9. Locate the name of your organization (your own institution, not DESC) under Organization.

    Note

    You may have to start typing the name in the text box to cause a drop-down menu to appear.

  10. Complete the rest of the form and click Ok at the top right of the page.

  11. After submitting, you will be asked to agree to the NERSC Appropriate Use Policy. This is required for your account request to proceed.

  12. Once your request is approved, you will receive an email with a link to set your initial password.

    Warning

    That link will expire if not used within 24 hours. Use the link promptly to set your password and Multi-Factor Authentication token.

Note

If you need assistance with your account request, contact lsst-desc-ops-mgmt at slac dot stanford dot edu
